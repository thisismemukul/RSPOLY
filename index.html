ABOUT RS POLY COMPOUNDS

Premium Plastic House “R.S.POLY COMPOUNDS” ‘(ISO 9001:2015 Certified Co.)”is Manufacturing & Compounding of Plastic Granules in India since 2011 as a manufacturer, trader and supplier of recycle plastic granules and recyclable plastic scrap, with a business vision and a green initiative to replace older methods of Plastic manufacturing motive to providing sustainable environment friendly plastic solutions for industrial usages. The latest twin screw technology ensures excellent filler dispersion to give goods quality compounds.

Our brand R.S.P.L is now word well spread all over the plastic industry in India and within a short- span of time our company has built good reputation in the market for excellent quality and timely delivery schedules.

R.S.POLY COMPOUNDS has grown into the market leader offering an extensive variety of reprocess plastic granules like PP GRANULES, HDPE GRANUELS, LLDPE GRANUELS, NYLON GRANULES, and ABS GRANULES.Our dedicated team of employees and professionals is the best natural resources for making reprocess plastics granules in it modern era. These reprocess plastic granules that find application in Auto motive plastic components,HDPE pipe, Plastic pallets, Plastic chairs, and House hold items, plastic compounding, ACP sheets, Plastic Air cooler and others.
